Introduction

At fidelit Wooden Cabinets, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data we obtain from our customers.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you place an order, we collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Information: We collect your payment information to process orders securely. We do not store your credit/debit card details.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience on our website. Cookies help us understand your preferences and tailor our services accordingly.

    • How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • To improve our customer service and overall Servicesping experience.
  • To notify you about your order status or provide updates about new services or promotions (only with your consent).

    • Data Security

    We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Your data is stored securely, and we only share it with trusted third-party service providers as necessary.
